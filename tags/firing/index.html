Coffe Cup

The idea for this project is pretty old and roots back to 2014. I decided to build a special shaped coffe cup, looking classic on the outside, but revealing its secret heart as the coffe level drops to half. What started as a simple birthday gift for my girlfriend, ended up as a 6 years long journey of ups and down,with some extraordinary long pauses as I did not know how to proceed.
